HI, I'M LAUREN!



I'm a Philadelphia based artist with a growing interest in web development & design!

In my art, I like creating colorful, cartoon-style illustrations, while my interest in tech leans towards making personal websites, exploring the web revival scene, and learning about design. Outside of art and tech, I'm passionate about music, nature, and cooking!

If you're interested in creating something together or just want to chat about web/art stuff, please feel free to reach out!


Web Design & Development Projects

I'm currently studying HTML and CSS through freecodecamp.org's Responsive Web Design course!

I decided to get into making websites as a hobby when I felt nostalgic for the personal websites of the early 2000s Internet/"Web 1.0". I like the idea of trying to blend that era's simplicity & creative self-expression with today's tech and accessibility practices.
