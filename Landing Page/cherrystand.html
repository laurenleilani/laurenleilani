The Cherry Stand



Nestled between The Able Sister's clothier and The Gyroid Cafe, MOONPRISM's Island Cherry Stand is conveniently located. Steps away from the Star-Gazing Bridge, stop by to try a variety of cherry-inspired treats crafted from the freshest ingredients harvested right here on MOONPRISM Island!

Our signature Cherry Smoothies are a must-try, blending sweet island-grown cherries with a hint of mint for a refreshing treat that's perfect for any time of day. And you won't want to miss our famous Cherry Pies, baked daily with a flaky crust and filled with juicy, tangy cherries that are sure to bring a smile to your face.

As the sun sets, join us for an unforgettable Cherry Tart under the shimmering stars, or sample our exclusive Cherry Jam, a perfect souvenir that captures the essence of our beautiful island.

Whether you're a local resident or just visiting, MOONPRISM's Cherry Stand is the perfect spot to relax, enjoy some delicious treats, and soak in the island's natural beauty!







MOONPRISM's Cherries


Grown right here on our island, MOONPRISM cherries are in season all year long, thanks to our unique microclimate and rich, fertile soil. Our orchards provide the best environment for growing the juiciest, most flavorful cherries you've ever tasted.

Our commitment to practicing sustainable farming practices ensures that each cherry is not only delicious but also cultivated with care for the environment. We use organic fertilizers and natural pest control methods to maintain the health and balance of our orchards.

Come visit, explore our island, and discover why our MOONPRISM cherries are so special!
